PERFORMANCE ASSURANCE PROGRAM

Our program helps to guarantee you performance cleaning services all the time. We tailor each program to your facilities requirements so we can meet your needs. Client satisfaction is our top priority and our program is designed to accomplish that. Our Performance Assurance Program is broken up into four stages.

DAILY TASK LIST

ISA provides its employees with a daily task list that details their regular duties. This ensures that our clients receive the level of service that they expect.

TIME LOG

Our employees clock in and out from your site via the telephone. This means that we can assure our clients that our employees are on site when scheduled.

TRAINING

All our employees are WHMIS, safety, and task trained. Our training is specific to your location, to ensure that your expectations are met. Additional training is provided when new procedures and equipment are available.

INSPECTIONS

An inspection checklist is developed with your company and your requirements in mind. Supervisors visit your site to complete the checklist with our employees. A copy of the inspection is available for you to review.
