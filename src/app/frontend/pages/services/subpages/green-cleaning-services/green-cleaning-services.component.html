GREEN CLEANING IS HEALTHY CLEANING

A green workplace has been found to be safer for employees and visitors alike. Also, in many instances, green cleaning processes are better for your facility.

For instance, regular cleaning agents tend to be caustic, wearing out surfaces over time and diminishing the longevity of your facility and your furnishings. Additionally, some cleaners can agitate those with sensitivities to chemicals.
